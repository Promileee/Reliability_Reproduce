# Reproduce of a Dynamic Mission Abort Policy via Deep Reinforcement Learning for Reliability Engineering Course
This repository is made to store codes and results while reproducing the paper from Reliability Engineering & System Safety. And a powerpoint for presentation is provided for this.
## Example Source
The paper is published on RESS, a top journal about industrial and engineering background. It build a squential decision making process at first, describe the failure of system and components via degradation modeling, and possess it as a Markov decision process. After that, they propose a DRL method to solve the MDP model, and compare the result with some heuristics methods.
> Liu, Lujie, Jun Yang, and Bingxin Yan. "A dynamic mission abort policy for transportation systems with stochastic dependence by deep reinforcement learning." Reliability Engineering & System Safety 241 (2024): 109682.
